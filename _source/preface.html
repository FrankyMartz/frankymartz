Preface

I find myself more fascinated in redesigning this website than crafting articles to supplement it. An ironic coincidence, considering web design is all about the content.

Don’t get me wrong, I love writing. The process of crafting statements, discussing philosophies, and offering opinions is nothing less than enjoyable. I simply can not deny the rush that precedes and succeeds a completed project (i.e. it feels great).

That said, from this point forward I plan on extending this site both vertically and horizontally; So, expect both.

Adventures guaranteed.

– Franky Martinez
